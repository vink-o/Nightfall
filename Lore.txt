The world survived wars.
The world survived disease.
It survived even its own mistakes.

Cities were rebuilt.
Robots assisted humans.
Energy flowed through the ruins of the old world.

Humanity believed the worst was behind them.

But they didn't know...

that someone was watching.

---

Every day, the survivors leave their shelters.
They search for energy.
They build bases.
They hope to rebuild civilization one day.

They think the day belongs to them.

But the night...

The night brings something else.

---

When the sun sets, the world changes.

Fog appears without warning.
Storms tear across the sky.
Something moves through the darkness.

Survivors call it NIGHTFALL.

No one knows what causes it.
No one knows why it happens.

But everyone knows one thing:

If you want to see the sunrise...

you must survive the night.
